# Sample Incident Report — Demo Document

**Incident ID:** INC-DEMO001
**Asset:** ASSET-247
**System:** Hydraulics
**Sub-system:** Actuator
**Severity:** High
**Date:** 2024-03-15

## Narrative

During scheduled 500-hour inspection of ASSET-247 hydraulic system at Bay A,
technician TECH-042 discovered a crack on the actuator rod at the aft attachment
point. The crack measured approximately 3.2mm in depth and was located at the
root fitting, consistent with fatigue loading from cyclic pressure operations.

Adjacent components showed early wear at the junction area. Previous inspection
300 flight hours ago noted no anomaly at this location. The finding was classified
as High severity per maintenance manual chapter 29.

Work order WO-48291 was raised and the unit was removed from service pending repair.
Component was tagged as unserviceable and submitted for metallurgical analysis.

## Corrective Action

Replaced the defective actuator assembly and conducted post-maintenance functional
test. Pressure tested to 150% working pressure. Returned to service after
satisfactory operational check. Fleet inspection of similar assets recommended.

## Notes

Similar finding reported on ASSET-183 two months prior. Root cause analysis
ongoing. Engineering reviewing hydraulic pressure profiles for fatigue assessment.
