# NCR-2024-0388: Avionics Display Unit Pixel Failure — EFIS PFD

**Report ID:** NCR-2024-0388  
**Date:** 2024-07-14  
**System:** Avionics / Flight Displays  
**Severity:** Major  
**Supplier:** AvioDisplay Systems  
**Part Number:** P/N PFD-737MAX-440  
**Aircraft:** Boeing 737 MAX 8, Tail N1103M  
**Engineer:** Carlos Mendez  

## Defect Description
Primary Flight Display (PFD) unit on Captain's side exhibited a 40mm x 12mm dead 
zone in the attitude indicator region during preflight checks. The dead zone 
caused incorrect rendering of pitch ladder at +2° to +4° pitch attitude. 
Crew correctly identified the fault and deferred the flight pending replacement. 
Total dark pixels: approximately 2,400 out of 2,073,600 (0.12% of display area), 
but located in a safety-critical zone affecting pitch attitude depiction.

## Root Cause
AvioDisplay Systems confirmed the display panel (lot AVD-2024-PFD-007) experienced 
early LCD backlight driver integrated circuit failure. The driver IC (AvioDisplay 
P/N BLD-IC-220) was procured from a secondary source following supply chain 
disruptions. Secondary-source ICs exhibited electrostatic discharge (ESD) sensitivity 
below the specified 2kV HBM threshold (measured: 800V HBM), resulting in latent 
damage to the gate driver layer during sub-assembly handling.

## Corrective Action
1. Replace PFD unit PFD-737MAX-440 (SN AVD-20240611)
2. Quarantine all PFD units from lot AVD-2024-PFD-007 — inspect before installation
3. AvioDisplay Systems to requalify secondary-source BLD-IC-220 to HBM Class 2 minimum
4. Incoming inspection protocol updated: add ESD sensitivity spot-check per MIL-STD-883
5. Issue SCAR-2024-0388 to AvioDisplay Systems — secondary-source component approval process
